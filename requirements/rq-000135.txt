title:: Represent a `Lookup' Statement

revision:: $Revision: 10973 $

date:: $Date: 2012-10-27 14:46:35 -0600 (Sat, 27 Oct 2012) $

author:: $Author: don.johnson $

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000073

type:: data format

associated:: rq-000078

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent a
`lookup' statement.  A lookup retrieves and inserts any division
property defined anywhere in the document.  The purpose of this
requirement is to maximize document maintainability by giving authors
the capability to define content in one location and reuse it in
multiple locations.
