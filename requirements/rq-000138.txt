>>>requirement.rq-000138

title:: Represent a `Status' Statement

revision:: $Revision: 10973 $

date:: $Date: 2012-10-27 14:46:35 -0600 (Sat, 27 Oct 2012) $

author:: $Author: don.johnson $

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000073

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to reference
an item's status.  The status of an item is the degree to which it is
complete and valid.  Status is one of: green, yellow, red, or grey.
The status of an item should be declared in one and only one location
then referenced in multiple locations. The purpose of this requirement
is to maximize document maintainability by giving authors the
capability to reference an item's definitive status in multiple
locations.

index:: [id:rq-000138]; status
