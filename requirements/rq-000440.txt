>>>requirement.rq-000440

title:: Satisfy Compliance Directives

type:: package

description:: The organization shall satisfy compliance directives.  A
compliance directive places constraints on how the organization
operates or on how systems are implemented and operated. This
requirement serves as a package (a container) for all compliance
directives levied on the organization.  Organizational activities are
often derived from compliance directives.

index:: rq-000440; compliance directives
