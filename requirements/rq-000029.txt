priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000195

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to identify and
represent document content as quotations.  Quotations are verbatim
replications of what another person said or wrote.  The purpose of
identifying content as a quotation is to make the content re-usable
and to enable special listings of quotations.  Furthermore, the data
format must provide the capability to capture specific elements of
meta data.

index:: [id:rq-000029]; quotations
