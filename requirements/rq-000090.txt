>>>requirement.rq-000090

title:: Define the Syntax for an Acronym Term Reference String

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000407

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent
an inline acronym term reference.  In other words, the data format
must have the capability to indicate an acronym is in the official
list of acronyms. The acronym `term' is the short form of the acronym
itself and not the expanded definition of the acronym.

index:: rq-000090; acronym term reference; string!acronym term
reference
