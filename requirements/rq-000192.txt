>>>requirement.rq-000192

title:: Emit a ~~Warning~~ Message On Finding ~~Minor~~ Invalid
First-Order Document Semantics

is_part_of:: rq-000203

type:: technical

description:: The software shall emit a ~~warning~~ message upon
finding ~~minor~~ invalid first-order document semantics.  A ~~minor~~
invalid semantics problem is one that results in erroneous or
misleading document content. First-order document semantics are
expressed as rules (axioms) in a [g:SML:library]'s [g:SML:ontology].
