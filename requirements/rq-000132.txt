>>>requirement.rq-000132

title:: Resolve Templates

is_part_of:: rq-000105

priority:: routine

type:: technical

description:: The software shall resolve template statements.
Template statements are a special type of include statement in which
one or more variable name/value pairs are passed and substituted in
the included division.  Templates are re-usable SML divisions
containing variable tags.  The template statement passes values for
those variables.  The software must replace the variable tags with the
values and replace the template statement with the template containing
the replaced variables.

index:: [id:rq-000132]; resolve templates
