>>>requirement.rq-000068

title:: Represent a Comment Division

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000066

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent a
comment division.  A comment division is multiple ~~whole~~ lines of
text that appear in the source data format but not in published
renditions.  The data format must provide a way to mark the beginning
and ending of a comment block without changing the lines within the
block.

index:: [id:rq-000068]; comment division
