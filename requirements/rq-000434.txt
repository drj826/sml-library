>>>requirement.rq-000434

title:: Describe How a Solution Works or Should Work

type:: activity

is_part_of:: rq-000355          # Design Solutions

description:: The organization shall describe ~~how~~ a solution works
or should work. The work product of this activity is a model of the
solution design. The model allocates functional requirements
(functions) to design components to explain how the design satisfies
requirements. The model also describes relationships between design
components.
