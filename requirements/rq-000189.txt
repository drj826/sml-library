>>>requirement.rq-000189

title:: Represent a Line-Ending Comment

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000066

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent a
line-ending comment.  Line-ending comments are ~~partial~~ lines of
text that appear in the source data format but not in published
renditions.  They begin at some point on a line and continue to the
end of the line.

index:: rq-000189; line-ending comment
