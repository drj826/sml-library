is_part_of:: rq-000107

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall distinguish reserved words from
non-reserved words.  The purpose of this requirement is to help users
distinguish reserved words from non-reserved words.  Reserved words
are terms with special meaning to the language itself that may ~~not~~
be re-used by users for their own purposes.
