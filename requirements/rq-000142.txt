>>>requirement.rq-000142

title:: Publish a Document Table of Contents

is_part_of:: rq-000139

type:: functional

description:: The software shall enable a user to generate a document
table of contents. The table of contents should include all front
matter parts that follows it but exclude any front matter parts that
follow it.  It should list the title and beginning page number of each
section of the book including the front matter, text divisions, and
back matter, including the index.  If the book is divided into parts
as well as chapters, the part titles appear in the contents, but their
page numbers are omitted.[cite:cms15, [section]1.41]

index:: rq-000142; table of contents
