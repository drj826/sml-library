>>>requirement.rq-000023

title:: Represent a Procedural Step

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000327

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent a
procedural step.  This capability enables an author to represent one
of potentially many steps within a sequence. The data format
representation of a procedural step must allow but not mandate newline
characters within a procedural step.

index:: rq-000023; procedural step
