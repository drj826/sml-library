>>>requirement.rq-000115

title:: Validate Requirements Documents

is_part_of:: rq-000200

priority:: routine

type:: functional

description:: The software shall enable a user to validate
requirements documents.  Validation means checking the document to
ensure it conforms to a specified set of rules.  Requirements
validation rules may specify that every requirement has specified
required attributes, is traceable to other requirements depending on
its type, etc.
