title:: Represent Glossary References

revision:: $Revision: 10973 $

date:: $Date: 2012-10-27 14:46:35 -0600 (Sat, 27 Oct 2012) $

author:: $Author: don.johnson $

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000009

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent
glossary references.  In other words, the data format must have the
capability to indicate an acronym, word, or phrase is in the
glossary.  The data format must allow glossary references to span
newline characters.
