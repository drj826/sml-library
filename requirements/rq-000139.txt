is_part_of:: rq-000199

priority:: routine

type:: technical

description:: The software shall generate document front and back
matter parts.  The interior of a printed book comprises three major
divisions: front matter, text, and back matter.  Some parts of the
front matter and back matter, like the table of contents, should be
automatically generated.  Other parts, like the preface, are written
manually by the author.  This requirement serves as a container for
software requirements to publish document front and back matter.

index:: [id:rq-000139]

According to The Chicago Manual of Style the following parts
generally, but not always, make up the front matter, text, and back
matter: [cite:cms15, [section]1.4]
