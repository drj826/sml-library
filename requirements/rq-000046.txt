>>>requirement.rq-000046

title:: Represent Fixed-Width Text

is_part_of:: rq-000120

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent
fixed-width text.  Fixed-width fonts feature characters that are all
the same width, even space characters, so plain text can be arranged
in columns.  Fixed-width is sometimes called ``typewriter text.''

index:: rq-000046; fixed-width text; text!fixed-width
