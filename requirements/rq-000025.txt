>>>requirement.rq-000025

title:: Represent a Glossary Entry

revision:: $Revision: 10973 $

date:: $Date: 2012-10-27 14:46:35 -0600 (Sat, 27 Oct 2012) $

author:: $Author: don.johnson $

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000327

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent a
glossary entry.  This capability enable an author to establish formal
definitions of terms used in a document.  The data format
representation of a glossary entry must allow but not mandate newline
characters within a glossary entry.

index:: [id:rq-000025]; glossary entry
