>>>requirement.rq-000162

title:: Represent an Option to Persist Generated Content In Document

revision:: $Revision: 11687 $

date:: $Date: 2012-12-06 13:59:01 -0700 (Thu, 06 Dec 2012) $

author:: $Author: don.johnson $

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000093

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent
the author's choice that generated content should persist in the
containing document.  The default behavior is to insert the generated
content into the output document but ~~not~~ persist it in the
original document.

index:: [id:rq-000162]; generated content!persistent
