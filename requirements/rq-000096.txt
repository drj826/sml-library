>>>requirement.rq-000096

title:: Represent a `Strict Entity Status' Option

revision:: $Revision: 10973 $

date:: $Date: 2012-10-27 14:46:35 -0600 (Sat, 27 Oct 2012) $

author:: $Author: don.johnson $

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000035

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent
the option of formally determining [g:SML2:entity] status from formal
outcomes rather than from informal entity status attributes.  The
purpose of this requirement is to enable authors to declare whether
they want entity status to come from formal test results and audits,
~~or~~ from the informal status attributes of each entity (default).

index:: [id:rq-000096]; status; item!status
