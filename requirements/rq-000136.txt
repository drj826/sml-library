>>>requirement.rq-000136

title:: Represent a `Definition Reuse' Statement

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000073

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent
definition re-use statements.  A definition retrieves and inserts any
glossary or acronym definition.  The purpose of this requirement is to
maximize document maintainability by giving authors the capability to
define terms and acronyms in one location and reuse them in multiple
locations.

index:: [id:rq-000136]; definitions
