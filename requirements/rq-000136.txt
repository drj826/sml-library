>>>requirement.rq-000136

title:: Represent a Definition Reference

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000120

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent a
definition reference.  A definition retrieves and inserts any glossary
or acronym definition.  The purpose of this requirement is to maximize
document maintainability by giving authors the capability to define
terms and acronyms in one location and reuse them in multiple
locations.

index:: rq-000136; definitions
