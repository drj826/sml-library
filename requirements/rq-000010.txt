>>>requirement.rq-000010

title:: Represent  a Paragraph

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000327

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent a
paragraph.  A paragraph is an element of document [g:SML1:structure],
a [g:SML:block] of [g:SML:text] that communicates an idea. The data
format representation of a paragraph must allow but not mandate
[g:SML:newline] characters within a paragraph.
