>>>requirement.rq-000191

title:: Emit ~~Error~~ Message On Finding ~~Major~~ Invalid
First-Order Document Semantics

is_part_of:: rq-000203

type:: technical

description:: The software shall emit an ~~error~~ message upon
finding ~~major~~ invalid first-order document semantics.  A ~~major~~
invalid semantics problem is one that prevents the production of
meaningful document output. First-order document semantics are
expressed as rules (axioms) in a [g:SML:library]'s [g:SML:ontology].
