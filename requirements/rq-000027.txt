priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000195

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to identify and
represent document content as exercises.  Exercises are instructions
to students to perform a series of tasks or solve one or problems to
reinforce learned concepts.  The purpose of identifying content as an
exercise is to make the content re-usable and to enable special
listings of exercises.
