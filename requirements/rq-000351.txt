>>>requirement.rq-000351

title:: Ignore Markup Inside Literal Text

is_part_of:: rq-000105

priority:: routine

type:: technical

description:: The software shall ignore markup inside literal text.
Literal text is a span of text in which all content is interpreted
literally.  This enables authors to add content that would otherwise
be interpreted as markup by the software.

index:: [id:rq-000351]
