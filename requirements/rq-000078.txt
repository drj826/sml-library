title:: Represent `Insert' Statements

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000073

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to insert
blocks of content defined elsewhere in the document.  A block of
content is one or more ~~whole~~ lines of text.

index:: [id:rq-000078]; inserts

Inserts are meant to represent ~~blocks~~ of content; that is, content
that consists of one or more ~~whole~~ lines of text.  Variables, on
the other hand, are meant to represent ~~inline~~ elements of content;
that is, content that potentially consists of ~~partial~~ lines of
text.
