>>>requirement.rq-000335

title:: Define the Syntax for a Section Heading

is_part_of:: rq-000327

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall define the syntax for a
[g:SML:SECTION] heading.  A section heading summarizes the topic of
the section and is used as an entry in the table of contents. The
section heading shall signify the beginning of the section. The syntax
shall capture the section heading and an optional unique identifier
for the section.

index:: rq-000335; section heading syntax; syntax!section heading

outcome::2016-03-02:rq-000335:green: This requirement is
complete. (Don Johnson)
