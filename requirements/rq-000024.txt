>>>requirement.rq-000024

title:: Represent a Side Bar

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000326

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent a
side bar. Side bar content is a small amount of information presented
in an emphasized way to reinforce points made in the main text.  The
data format representation of a side bar must allow but not mandate
newline characters within a side bar.

index:: rq-000024; side bar
