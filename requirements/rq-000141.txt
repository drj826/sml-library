description:: The software shall enable a user to generate a copyright
page.  The copyright page displays a biographical note, publisher's
address, copyright notice, copyright owner versus publisher, year of
publication, subsequent editions, additional copyright information,
publishing history, edition number, country of printing, impression,
translation, international standard book number (ISBN), International
Standard Serial Number (ISSN), acknowledgments, permissions, other
credits, grants, Cataloging-in-Publication (CIP) data, and paper
durability statement.
