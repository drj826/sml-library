>>>requirement.rq-000141

title:: Publish a Document Copyright Page

is_part_of:: rq-000139

priority:: routine

type:: functional

description:: The software shall enable a user to generate a document
copyright page.  The copyright page displays a biographical note,
publisher's address, copyright notice, copyright owner versus
publisher, year of publication, subsequent editions, additional
copyright information, publishing history, edition number, country of
printing, impression, translation, international standard book number
(ISBN), International Standard Serial Number (ISSN), acknowledgments,
permissions, other credits, grants, Cataloging-in-Publication (CIP)
data, and paper durability statement.

index:: [id:rq-000141]; copyright page
