title:: Represent an Acronym Definition

revision:: $Revision: 10973 $

date:: $Date: 2012-10-27 14:46:35 -0600 (Sat, 27 Oct 2012) $

author:: $Author: don.johnson $

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000327

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent
an acronym definition.  This capability enables an author to establish
formal definitions of acronyms used in a document.  The data format
representation of an acronym definition must allow but not mandate
newline characters within an acronym definition.
