>>>requirement.rq-000089

title:: Represent an Acronym List Entry

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000327

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent
an acronym list entry (acrony/definition pair).  This capability
enables an author to establish formal definitions of acronyms used in
a document.  The data format representation of an acronym definition
must allow but not mandate newline characters within an acronym
definition.

index:: [id:rq-000089]; acronym; acronym list!entry

<<<requirement
