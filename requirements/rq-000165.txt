>>>requirement.rq-000165

title:: Represent an Option to `Include As Section'

is_part_of:: rq-000065

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent
an `include' statement where the included content must be included
~~as a section of specified level~~. Convert the title of the external
file to a section heading at a specified section depth.  Make all
sections and sub-sections from the external file relative to the
specified section depth.

index:: rq-000165; include!as section
