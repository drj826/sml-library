priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000195

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to identify and
represent document content as a list of key points.  Key point lists
are summarizations of a series of key concepts within a document
section.  The purpose of identifying content as a list of key points
is to enable a concise summary of concepts, make the list of key
points re-usable, and to enable special listings of key points lists.

index:: [id:rq-000031]; key points; list of key points
