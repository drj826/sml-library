title:: Represent Templates

author:: $Author: don.johnson $

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000009

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent
templates.  Templates enable an author to create reusable blocks of
document structure and format that get filled in with reusable block
or inline elements of content.

index:: [id:rq-000077]; templates

For instance, an author might want to create a template for a
requirement (aka problem) that summarizes selected requirement
meta-data.
