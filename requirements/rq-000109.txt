>>>requirement.rq-000109

title:: Ignore Comments

is_part_of:: rq-000105

priority:: routine

type:: technical

description:: The software shall ignore comments.  This means that
lines of text in an SML file that are either (1) in a comment division
or (2) individually commented out should be ignored for the purposes
of gathering structured data from the document and publishing document
renditions.

index:: rq-000109; comments!ignore
