>>>requirement.rq-000146

title:: Publish a Document List of Abbreviations

is_part_of:: rq-000139

type:: functional

description:: The software shall enable a user to generate a document
list of abbreviations. The list may go in either the front matter or
the back matter.  If abbreviations are used in the text or in
footnotes, the list should appear in the front matter. If they are
only used in the back matter, the list should appear before the first
part in which abbreviations are used, whether the appendixes, the
end-notes, or the bibliography.

index:: rq-000146; list of abbreviations
