>>>requirement.rq-000353

title:: Represent Strikethrough Text

priority:: routine

is_part_of:: rq-000120

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent
strikethrough text.  Strikethrough (also called strikeout) is a
typographical presentation of words with a horizontal line through
their center.  The words remain readable.  This presentation
signifies, (1) the words are a mistake and not meant for inclusion,
(2) the author implies a change of thought but wants the reader to see
the original thought, or (3) [g:SML:epanorthosis].

index:: rq-000060; strikethrough text; strikeout text;
epanorthosis
